About EDRP

Enterprise Distribution & Resource Partners (EDRP) operates as an independent intermediary between industrial supply and operational demand.

We work with equipment owners, dealers, resellers, rental operators, service providers, and industrial end users to facilitate the movement of critical infrastructure assets across North and South America.

What We Do

Asset Brokerage

Representation and sourcing of generators, engines, industrial motors, and heavy equipment.

Market Intelligence

Monitoring surplus inventory, industrial demand, pricing trends, and equipment availability.

Distribution Support

Buyer identification, documentation coordination, and transaction facilitation.

Cross-Border Transactions

Supporting asset movement throughout Canada, the United States, Latin America, and the Caribbean.

Industries Served

Power Generation

Diesel, gas, standby, and prime power systems.

Mining & Resources

Remote operations and infrastructure support.

Industrial Manufacturing

Production facilities and process industries.

Marine & Offshore

Marine engines and auxiliary power systems.

Infrastructure & Utilities

Municipal, institutional, and utility projects.

Partnership Approach

EDRP is not a competing dealer.

We work alongside equipment dealers, resellers, rental fleets, service providers, and industrial operators to expand transaction opportunities and improve asset visibility across markets.

Our role is to identify opportunities, connect qualified parties, and support successful transactions.
